Note that the
while

Do while

Do while is similar to while loop, as you can probably already knew from it's name. Indeed, there is only one difference between
do while
and
while
. Do while will executed the body of the loop first, and then test the conditions.

Break and continue

There are two special operations can be used in any of the loops,
break
and
continue
.
break
During a loop, you can use the
break
statement to force stop the current loop.
During a loop, you can use the
continue
statement to ignore the current iteration, and force move on to the next iteration by ignoring the remaining operations in the loop.
